# AD-Genomics-Cardiff

Team website for AD-Genomics-Cardiff at the UK Dementia Research Institute (Cardiff).

This site presents our work on integrative genetic and multi-omics analysis for Alzheimer’s disease, including GWAS, polygenic risk, long-read sequencing, and pathway-based analyses.

## 🧠 About

The AD-Genomics-Cardiff team focuses on understanding the genetic and molecular mechanisms of Alzheimer’s disease through integrative analysis across multiple data types.
